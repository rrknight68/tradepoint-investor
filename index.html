Which Tranche Fits Your Capital

Senior Pref — $4M @ 13%
Fixed yield, senior position
13% simple interest on $4M over 36 months. Total return: $5,560,000 · 1.39× EM. Paid before equity distributions. No profit participation — fully predictable return. Ideal for capital seeking yield certainty over speculative upside.
Co-GP Equity — $3.24M · 50/50
Maximum upside participation
10% preferred return + 50% of all profit above pref. 39.1% IRR · 2.69× EM · $8,694,126 total return. Amplified by the smaller equity base against the full project profit pool. Ideal for capital seeking top-of-market returns on a short industrial hold.

Why Trade Point

SBA 504 exit — de-risked absorption
Every buyer enters with institutional financing at 10% down. 38 independent owner-user closings — no bulk sale dependency, no single point of failure.
Phase 2 fully self-funded
Phase 1 generates $8.04M net after loan repayment — fully covers both Phase 2 equity tranches with a $5.41M surplus. No second capital call, ever.
Supply-constrained SWFL market
Southwest Florida flex industrial is chronically undersupplied. Cape Coral's industrial base has grown significantly with demand outpacing new deliveries.
Conservative pricing with built-in escalation
Phase 1 at $350/SF is the base case. Phases 2A and 2B escalate to $357.50 and $367.50 as market comps accumulate through Phase 1 sell-out.
Returns above market benchmark
Flex industrial Co-GP deals typically target 18–25% IRR. The equity tranche delivers 39.1% at the base case without stretching absorption assumptions.
Short hold, clear exit path
36-month absorption-driven exit. Each unit closes independently through SBA 504 — no refinance dependency, no bulk discount risk.
